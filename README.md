## Visual Identity
- **Accent**: Warm rust/terracotta (hue 15, sat 72) — artisan craft warmth, desert palette
- **Secondary**: Golden blonde (#d4a843) — Silver State Blonde, Nevada gold
- **Display Font**: Manrope (geometric humanist) | **Body Font**: Space Grotesk (modern monoline)
- **Tagline**: "Building Community, One Pint at a Time."
- **Loyalty Currency**: Haus Points

## Key Brand Details
- Founded 2014 by Wyndee and Dave Forrest — partners in business and life
- Inspired by European beer culture during travels
- Two locations: Henderson production brewery (7350 Eastgate Rd) + Arts District taproom (197 E California St)
- Nevada's Greenest Brewery, Best Family Owned Business (Nevada Entrepreneur Magazine)
- UNLV partnership — Rebel Spirit is the official beer of UNLV
- Advocacy for Nevada Independent Brewers at state and national level
- Spent grain pretzels with beer cheese, beer brats (The Beast: wild boar/elk/wagyu)
- Dog-friendly Arts District patio
- Free Saturday tours at 2 PM with tastings
- 532 beers catalogued on Untappd, 27 medals (7 gold, 13 silver, 7 bronze)
